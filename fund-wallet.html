Let's fund your Abstract wallet

It's easier than you think! Go to your Abstract profile (https://portal.abs.xyz/profile) and find the +Fund button. Then choose your funding method; the easiest option is through Moonpay with Apple Pay or your bank card.

If you already have cryptocurrency, you can use: withdrawal from a centralized exchange, transfer from another wallet, or bridging from another network. Remember that the Abstract wallet address only accepts funds in the Abstract network!
