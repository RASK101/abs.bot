Let's set up an Abstract wallet

Create Your Abstract Wallet

It's easier than you think! Go to the Abstract login page (https://portal.abs.xyz/login) and sign in with your email or Google account, just like on any other site. You can also register using your EVM wallet.

*Note that a different wallet address will be generated in the Abstract network, so consider this when transferring funds to Abstract!*

Do you need help funding your Abstract wallet?

Fund Your Abstract Wallet

It's easier than you think! Go to your Abstract profile (https://portal.abs.xyz/profile) and find the +Fund button. Then choose your funding method; the easiest option is through Moonpay with Apple Pay or your bank card. If you already have cryptocurrency, you can use: withdrawal from a centralized exchange, transfer from another wallet, or bridging from another network.

*Remember that the Abstract wallet address only accepts funds in the Abstract network!*
